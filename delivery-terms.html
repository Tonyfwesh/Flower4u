Delivery Terms

We strive to provide a smooth and efficient delivery experience. Please review our delivery terms:

  • Delivery is available within Lusaka and surrounding areas.
  • Orders must be placed by 2 PM for same-day delivery.
  • A delivery fee may apply based on the distance and time of delivery.
  • We guarantee fresh flowers and timely delivery. However, specific delivery times cannot be guaranteed due to unforeseen circumstances.
  • If the recipient is unavailable at the time of delivery, the flowers may be left with a neighbor or returned to our shop for rescheduling.

For any specific delivery instructions or concerns, please contact our customer service team at contact us.
