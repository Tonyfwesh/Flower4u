About Us

Our journey began with a passion for flowers and a desire to share their beauty with others. Over the years, we have become known for our stunning arrangements and exceptional customer service. We pride ourselves on offering fresh, high-quality blooms and unique designs that add a special touch to any event or moment.



As we continue to grow, we're excited to expand our services and reach more customers through our website, Flowers4U.co.zm. Now, you can easily explore our selection of flowers and bouquets, and have them delivered right to your door or the door of your loved ones. Our online store makes it simple and convenient to choose the perfect floral gift, no matter where you are.


At Flowers4U, we're more than just a florist. We're a team of passionate flower enthusiasts who are dedicated to making your special moments even more memorable. We look forward to helping you find the perfect flowers to celebrate life's beautiful occasions.


Thank you for choosing Flowers4U. We can't wait to bring a smile to your face with our beautiful blooms!

Our Mission

At Flowers4U, our mission is to create memorable experiences through the art of floral design.
